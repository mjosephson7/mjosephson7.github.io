Pass & Play

No extra devices needed. Just one phone, a group of friends, and a whole lot of suspicion.

How It Works

1

Pass the Phone

Each player peeks at their screen privately — most see the secret word, one sees nothing.

2

Ask Questions

Players question each other about the word. The Quack has to bluff their way through.

3

Vote & Reveal

Everyone votes on who they think is the Quack. Then the truth comes out.

4

Play Again

Roles shuffle every round. Anyone can be the Quack next.
